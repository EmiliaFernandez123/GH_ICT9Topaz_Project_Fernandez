According to The Zebra 2,600+ people die due to home fires each year, and being in the month of march, this would be the perfect time to spread awareness and precautions to take to avoid these accidents as much as possible.



Be attentive and extra careful when using the stove to cook. Make sure you turn it on properly and turn it completely off after use.
Install the basic fire prevention items in your home. (Fire alarms, fire extuinguisher and easy escape passage)
Store flammable products or materials in a secure place far from possible direct fire.
Turn off and/or unplug electrical equipment at home that isn't being used.
Avoid smoking indoors. If one does smoke, make sure it's in a well ventilated area as to avoid contact with easily flammable materials.
